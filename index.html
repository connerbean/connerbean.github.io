About Me




Hello, I'm Conner. I am an enthused soon-to-be super senior at Michigan State University, as well as an avid Sci-Fi fan, a lover of nature, and best friend to my dog. I love creating things, whether it be hands-on or virtual, I appreciate being fortunate enough to make things day in and day out. This past summer, as well as currently, I have been fortunate enough to work with PS Technology in their Okemos, MI office as a Simulation Software Intern. Prior to that, I was a mathematics tutor for over 4 years in courses ranging from Pre-Calculus to Calculus III, and a CS tutor for 2 years in C++/Python Programming. Evidently by my long career in tutoring, a big passion of mine is learning. I love to learn myself, and actively try each day to learn something new and fascinating to me. More so, I love to help others learn. In my opinion, one of the biggest satisfactions in life comes from helping someone understand something they previously thought they couldn't. Following this idea, I am very pleased to mention that for the Spring Semester of 2018, I will be a CSE331: Data Structures & Algorithms TA under Dr. Sebnem Onsay. I very much look forward to helping students learn and grow throughout the course.
